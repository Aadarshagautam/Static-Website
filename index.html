About the Companys

We are a dynamically developing company offering comprehensive services in the field of management and management of projects in the telecommunications, data communications and electrical industries. We have extensive experience through which we provide services at a high level. The company has existed since 2016, and during this time we have based more than 100 project documentation.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

  1. preparation of assumptions and pre-project concepts
  2. valuation of construction investments
  3. advisory on selection of technologies
  4. consulting on classified systems
  5. advisory on selection of technologies
  6. Values of word change code

  5. evaluation of physical and technical security of premises where classified information is processed
